About me

I'm a third-year BSIT student at USTP with a strong foundation in IT and a growing passion for design and user experience. I enjoy learning new technologies, collaborating on projects, and constantly improving my skills.

One of my key strengths is UI/UX design. I enjoy creating intuitive, user-centered interfaces that balance function and aesthetics. With a keen eye for detail, I continuously explore design tools and trends to refine my skills.

Lately, I’ve also been diving deep into the exciting world of artificial intelligence and full-stack development. Exploring how AI can be integrated into everyday applications has sparked a new wave of curiosity and motivation in me. I’ve been dedicating time to understand both the front-end and back-end processes of building web and mobile apps, learning how design, functionality, and data all come together to form powerful digital solutions. This growing interest pushes me to keep experimenting with code, study frameworks, and understand how intelligent systems work behind the scenes.

Outside of my academic and design pursuits, I am an avid mobile gamer. I particularly enjoy playing League of Legends: Wild Rift, where I’ve developed strategic thinking, quick decision-making, and teamwork—skills that I find surprisingly valuable in both gaming and real-life scenarios. Whether I’m designing a user interface, writing lines of code, or leading a match in the Rift, I bring passion, focus, and a drive to continuously improve. Looking ahead, I aspire to grow as a tech professional who combines innovation, creativity, and functionality to make meaningful digital experiences.
